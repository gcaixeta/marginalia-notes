### Define the today task

In the morning, the life app asks you for the day tasks. Then we can populate de to-do list for the day and, whenever you end a task, it can offer you another one from the list. Whenever you add a task, it asks for the priority. That way, it offers you the most urgents ones first, following the idea that we should take the hardest tasks first in the morning.
